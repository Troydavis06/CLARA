How It Works
Ingest
Parses Bandit, ZAP, npm audit & osv-scanner outputs into normalized Finding objects.
Deduplicate
Removes findings with identical CWE + location across tools, keeping highest severity.
Cluster
Gemini assigns each finding to one of 7 attack surface categories.
Synthesize
Gemini builds named attack chains spanning multiple surfaces with business impact.
MITRE Map
Gemini assigns ATT&CK techniques per chain with confidence scores & fix priority.
Output
Prioritized attack chains ready for developer remediation.
